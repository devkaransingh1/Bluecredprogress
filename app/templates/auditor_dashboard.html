AUDITOR RESPONSIBILITIES
  • 01
    Verify site credentials before entry

    Confirm the Site Access Code matches the official assignment letter from BlueCred before proceeding with inspection.

  • 02
    Collect readings independently

    All pollution readings must be taken by you using calibrated instruments. Do not rely on data provided by the industry.

  • 03
    Attach supporting field evidence

    Photographs, instrument logs, and site sketches must be attached before finalising the report.

  • 04
    Submit within 24 hours of inspection

    Reports must be filed the same day or the next morning. Delayed submissions require admin approval.

⚠Legal Notice

Falsifying, omitting, or manipulating data in an audit report is a criminal offence under the Environment Protection Act, 1986. All submissions are permanently recorded and subject to regulatory review.
