Forgot Your Credentials?

BlueCred auditor credentials are issued and managed exclusively by the BlueCred team. If you have lost access to your Blue ID or Blue Password, you must contact our support department directly to have them reissued.

⛔
Manual password reset is not permitted.

Self-service password reset is intentionally disabled on BlueCred. Because auditor credentials are tied to verified identities and carry legal accountability for submitted data, only the BlueCred admin team may reissue or reset them — after re-confirming your identity.

HOW TO RECOVER YOUR CREDENTIALS
01
Contact the BlueCred helpdesk

Reach out via email or phone using the details below. Include your registered name and organisation.

02
Identity re-verification

The team will verify your identity against the original onboarding records before proceeding.

03
New credentials issued

Fresh credentials will be sent to your official registered email. This typically takes 1–2 business days.
